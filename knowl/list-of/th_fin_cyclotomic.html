Theorem 1.19.11. Finite fields are cyclotomic.

Any finite extension of finite fields is cyclotomic.

Proof.

The argument above proves the result in the special case of extensions of the form \(K/\F_{p}\text{.}\) For the more general case \(L/K\text{,}\) note that we have \(L/K/\F_p\) for some prime integer \(p\text{.}\) The special case then gives us \(L=\F_p(\alpha)=K(\alpha)\) for some \(n\)-th root of unity \(\alpha\text{.}\)
