Theorem 1.21.9. Galois group of quartic.

Let \(F\) be a field of characteristic not equal to \(2\text{,}\) and let \(f(x)\) be a monic separable irreducible polynomial of degree \(4\text{.}\) Using the notation of Theorem 1.21.6, we have the following equivalences:
\begin{align} G_f\cong S_4 \amp \iff \sqrt{\Delta_f}\notin F \text{ and } R_f(x) \text{ is irreducible}\tag{1.39}\\ G_f\cong A_4 \amp \iff \sqrt{\Delta_f}\in F \text{ and } R_f(x) \text{ is irreducible}\tag{1.40}\\ G_f\cong C_4 \text{ or } G_f\cong D_4 \amp \iff \sqrt{\Delta_f}\notin F \text{ and } R_f(x) \text{ is reducible} \tag{1.41}\\ G_f\cong V \amp \iff \sqrt{\Delta_f}\in F \text{ and } R_f(x) \text{ is reducible}\tag{1.42} \end{align}

Proof.

Let \(G_f\cong H\leq S_4\) via the map in Theorem 1.20.3. Summarizing our discussion above, we have
\begin{align*} H\in \{S_4,A_4} \amp \iff 3\mid \abs{H} \\ \amp \iff R_f(x) \text{ irreducible}\text{.} \end{align*}
This, coupled with the equivalence \(H\leq A_4\) if and only if \(\sqrt{\Delta_f}\in F\text{,}\) yields the first two equivalences of the theorem. The last two statements follow in a similar manner.
