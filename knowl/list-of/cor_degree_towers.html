Corollary 1.5.8. Tower law.

Let \(L/F\) be a field extension.
  1. If \(K/F\) is a subextension of \(L/F\text{,}\) then \([K\colon F]\mid [L\colon F]\) and \([L\colon K]\mid [L\colon F]\text{.}\)
  2. Given a tower of extensions
    \begin{equation*} F_1\leq F_2\leq \cdots \leq F_n\text{,} \end{equation*}
    we have
    \begin{equation*} [F_n\colon F_1]=\prod_{k=1}^{n-1}[F_{k+1}\colon F_k]\text{.} \end{equation*}
