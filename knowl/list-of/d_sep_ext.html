Definition 1.13.1. Separable extension.

Let \(K/F\) be a field extension. An element \(\alpha\in K\) is separable over \(F\) if and only if it satisfies a separable polynomial \(f\in F[x]\text{.}\)
The extension \(K\) is separable over \(F\) if all elements of \(K\) are separable over \(F\text{.}\)
