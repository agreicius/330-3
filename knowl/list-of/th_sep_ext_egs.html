Theorem 1.13.5. Separable families of extensions.

Let \(K/F\) be an algebraic field extension.
  1. If \(\character F=0\text{,}\) then \(K\) is separable over \(F\text{.}\)
  2. If \(\character F=p> 0\) and the Frobenius map \(a\mapsto a^p\) is surjective on \(F\text{,}\) then \(K\) is separable over \(F\text{.}\)

Proof.

These are immediate consequences of Corollary 1.12.11–1.12.12, Definition 1.13.1, and Theorem 1.13.3.
