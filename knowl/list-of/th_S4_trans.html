Theorem 1.21.6. Transitive subgroups of \(S_4\).

The following is a complete list of the transitive subgroups of \(S_4\text{.}\)
  1. Permutation group \(S_4\).
    The entire group \(S_4\text{.}\)
  2. Alternating subgroup \(A_4\).
    The alternating subgroup \(A_4\text{,}\) which is normal in \(S_4\text{.}\)
  3. Klein-4 group \(V\normalin A_4\).
    A unique isomorphic copy of the Klein-4 group of the form \(V=\angvec{(12)(34),(13)(24)}\text{,}\) which is a normal subgroup of \(A_4\text{.}\)
  4. Cyclic group \(C_4\).
    Three isomorphic copies of the cyclic group \(C_4\) of the form \(\angvec{(abcd)}\text{,}\) all of which are conjugate to one another.
  5. Dihedral group \(D_4\).
    Three isomorphic copies of \(D_4\) of the form \(\angvec{(abcd),(ac)}\text{,}\) all of which are conjugate to one another.
