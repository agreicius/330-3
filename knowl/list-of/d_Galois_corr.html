Definition 1.16.4. Galois correspondence.

Let \(L/F\) be a Galois extension. Let \(\mathcal{F}\) be the set of all intermediate fields of \(L/F\text{,}\) and let \(\mathcal{S}\) be the set of all subgroups of \(\Gal(L/F)\text{.}\) The Galois correspondence of \(L/F\) is the pair of maps
\begin{align*} \mathcal{F} \amp \rightarrow \mathcal{S} \amp \mathcal{S}\amp \rightarrow \mathcal{F}\\ K \amp \longmapsto \Aut_K(L) \amp H\amp\longmapsto L^H\text{.} \end{align*}
