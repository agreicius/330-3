Corollary 1.4.15. Minimal poylnomial.

Let \(K/F\) be a field extension, and let \(\alpha\in K\) be an algebraic element with minimal polynomial \(m_{\alpha}(x)\text{.}\)
  1. Let \(n=\deg m_\alpha(x)\text{.}\) The set \(B=\{1,\alpha, \dots, \alpha^{n-1}\}\) is an \(F\)-basis of \(F(\alpha)\text{,}\) and thus \([F(\alpha)\colon F]=n\text{.}\)
  2. Given \(f\in F[x]\text{,}\) we have \(f(\alpha)=0\) if and only if \(m_{\alpha, F}(x)\mid f(x)\text{.}\) In particular, if \(f(\alpha)=0\text{,}\) then \(m_{\alpha,F}(x)\) is one of the monic irreducible factors of \(f(x)\text{.}\)
