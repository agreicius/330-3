Theorem 1.20.8. Discriminant and alternating group.

Let \(f(x)\in F[x]\) be a separable irreducible polynomial. Denote by \(\sqrt{\Delta_f}\) a square-root of \(\Delta_f\) lying in an algebraic closure \(\overline{F}\text{.}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle \Delta_f\in F\)
  2. Assume \(\character F\ne 2\text{.}\) Let \(H=\phi(G_f)\) be the isomorphic image of \(G_f\) in \(S_n\) under the homomorphism \(\phi\) of TheoremÂ 1.20.3. We have
    \begin{equation*} \sqrt{\Delta_f}\in F \iff H\leq A_n\text{.} \end{equation*}
