Theorem 1.21.13. \(D_4\) or \(C_4\).

Let \(F\) be a field of characteristic not equal to \(2\text{,}\) and let \(f(x)\) be a monic separable irreducible polynomial of degree \(4\text{.}\) Suppose that \(\sqrt{\Delta_f}\notin F\) and that \(R_f(x)\) has a unique root \(r\in F\text{.}\) Letting \(f(x)=x^4+ax^3+bx^2+cx+d\text{,}\) and using the notation of TheoremÂ 1.21.6, we have
\begin{equation} G_f\cong \begin{cases}C_4 \amp \text{if } x^2+ax+(b-r) \text{ and } x^2-rx+d \text{ split over } F(\sqrt{\Delta_f}) \\ D_4 \amp \text{otherwise.} \end{cases}\tag{1.44} \end{equation}
