Theorem 1.21.1. Galois group of cubic.

Let \(F\) be a field of cardinality not equal to \(2\text{,}\) and let \(f(x)\in F[x]\) be a separable irreducible polynomial of degree \(3\text{.}\)
  1. \(G_f\cong A_3\) or \(G_f\cong S_3\text{.}\)
  2. \(G_f\cong A_3\) if and only if \(\sqrt{\Delta_f}\in F\text{.}\)

Proof.

The result follows directly from Theorem 1.20.3, Theorem 1.20.9, and the fact that the only transitive subgroups of \(S_3\) are \(A_3\) and \(S_3\text{.}\)
