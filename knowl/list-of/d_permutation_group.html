Definition 1.20.2. Permutation group.

Let \(X=\{1,2,\dots, n\}\) and let \(S_n=S_X\text{,}\) the group of permutations of \(X\text{.}\) A permutation group is a subgroup \(H\) of \(S_n\text{.}\) A permutation group \(H\leq S_n\) is transitive if for any pair \(i\ne j\in X\) there is a \(\sigma\in H\) such that \(\sigma(i)=j\text{:}\) i.e., if the action of \(H\) on \(X\) is transitive.
