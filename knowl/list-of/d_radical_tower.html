Definition 1.22.5. Radical towers.

Let \(F\) be a field. A radical tower over \(F\) is a tower of fields
\begin{equation} F=F_0\leq F_1\leq \cdots \leq F_n=L\tag{1.47} \end{equation}
where for all \(1\leq k\leq n\text{,}\) \(F_k/F_{k-1}\) is a simple radical extension.
A root extension over \(F\) is a field extension \(L/F\) containing a radical tower as in (1.47).
