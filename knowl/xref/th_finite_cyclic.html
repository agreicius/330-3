Theorem 1.9.7. Finite fields are simple.

If \(K/\F_p\) is a finite extension, then \(K=\F_p(\alpha)\) for some \(\alpha\in \Fpbar\text{.}\)

Proof.

Since \(K^*\) is cyclic, we have \(K^*=\langle \alpha\rangle=\{1, \alpha, \alpha^2,\dots\}\) for some \(\alpha\in K\text{.}\) It follows easily that \(K=F(\alpha)\text{.}\)
