Definition 1.4.11. Algebraic and transcendental elements.

Let \(K/F\) be a field extension. An element \(\alpha\in K\) is algebraic over \(F\) if there is a nonzero polynomial \(f(x)\in F[x]\) such that \(f(\alpha)=0\text{;}\) an element \(\alpha\in K\) is transcendental over \(F\) if it is not algebraic over \(F\text{.}\)
