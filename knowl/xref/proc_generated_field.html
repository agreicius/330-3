Procedure 1.2.7. Generated field.

Given an extension \(F\leq K\) and subset \(A\subseteq K\text{,}\) to prove \(F(A)=L\text{,}\) proceed as follows:
  1. \(L\) contains \(F\) and \(A\).
    Show that \(L\) is a subfield of \(K\) containing \(F\) and \(A\text{.}\)
  2. \(L\) is the smallest such field.
    Show that for any subfield \(K'\) of \(K\) containing \(F\) and \(A\text{,}\) we have \(L\subseteq K'\text{.}\)
