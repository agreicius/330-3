Definition 1.20.4. Discriminant of polynomial.

Let \(f(x)\in F[x]\) be a polynomial of degree \(n\text{,}\) let \(\Fbar/F\) be an algebraic closure, and assume \(f\) factors over \(\Fbar\) as
\begin{equation*} f(x)=(x-\alpha_1)(x-\alpha_2)\cdots (x-\alpha_n)\text{.} \end{equation*}
The discriminant of \(f\), denoted \(\Delta_f\) is defined as
\begin{equation} \Delta_f=\prod_{1\leq i< j\leq n}(\alpha_j-\alpha_i)^2\text{.}\tag{1.36} \end{equation}
