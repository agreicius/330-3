Item. Field of rational functions.
Let \(F\) be a field, and let \(R=F[x_1,x_2,\dots, x_n]\) be the ring of polynomials over \(F\) in \(n\) variables. We denote by \(F(x_1,x_2,\dots, x_n)\) the corresponding field of rational functions: i.e.,
\begin{equation*} F(x_1,x_2,\dots, x_n)=\{f/g \colon f,g\in R, g\ne 0\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
This is the fraction field \(\Frac R\) of the polynomial ring \(R\text{.}\)
